His additional film credits include "Santa Sangre," 
"Second Best," "Jack & Sarah," "Shallow Grave," 
"Dust Devil," "The Turn of the Screw" and 
"Aquarius."

In addition to performing and recording his own 
music, Boswell has written and produced material 
for an eclectic assortment of musical artists, 
including Daisy Chain, 23 Skidoo, Pierce Turner, Nik
Kershaw, Amii Stewart, Live Wire, Mark Andrews 
and The Crooks.  He is also a regular contributor to 
Sight and Sound Magazine.
